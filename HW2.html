Select only the necessary columns, rename those columns and also add below columns: -date:Date without hours -weekDay:Day of Week -hour:Hour of the day Also, in order to have more descriptive graphs define data_longer dataset.

data<-rawData %>% 
  select(dt=Tarih,MCP=PTF,SMP=SMF)%>% 
  arrange(dt)%>% 
  mutate(dateTime=as.POSIXct(factor(dt), format="%d.%m.%y %H:%M")) %>%
  transmute(dateTime, date=as_date(dateTime), weekDay = wday(dateTime,label = TRUE,week_start=1) ,
            hour = hour(dateTime),MCP,SMP, Difference =(MCP-SMP))
